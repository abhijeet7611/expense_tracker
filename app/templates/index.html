Savings & Investment Hub

1. Audit Subscriptions

Scan your monthly recurring charges. Cancel anything you haven't used in the past 30 days.

2. The 72-Hour Rule

Before making any non-essential purchase, wait 72 hours. This eliminates impulse shopping.

3. Reduce Utilities Cost

Turn off background electronics, lower thermostat values by a few degrees, and check for draft leaks.

Medium Risk

Index Funds & ETFs
